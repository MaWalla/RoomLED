ROOMLED

Cheat Sheet

Keys are 'leds1' 'leds2' and 'leds3 for their respective strips. Values can be as follows:

  • {0:[r,g,b], 1:[r,g,b], ...} for each led in the strip to assign an individual colour value

  • '#abcdef' or [128, 32, 48] to colour the entire strip in 1 colour

  • 'random' well, it does what you'd expect. Each run presents a new colourful result!
